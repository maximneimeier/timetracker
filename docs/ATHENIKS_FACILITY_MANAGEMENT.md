# Atheniks Facility Management — Industry Solution

**Tagline:** Financial management for property and facility operators. Operating costs, owner reserves, and maintenance planning.

## Overview

Atheniks Facility Management is a financial management solution designed for property managers, facility operators, and real estate companies. It provides comprehensive tools for tracking operating costs, managing owner reserves, analyzing reserve adequacy, and ensuring proper financial planning for maintenance and capital expenditures.

## Target Market

**Industry:** Facility Management, Property Management, Real Estate  
**Property Types:** Commercial buildings, residential complexes, mixed-use properties  
**Company Size:** Mid-size operators (5-100 properties)  
**Geography:** Germany (DACH-focused)  
**Users:** Facility managers, property accountants, asset managers, owners' associations

## Core Functions

| Function | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Operating Cost Management** | Track and allocate operating expenses per property/unit |
| **Owner Reserve Management** | Calculate, collect, and track owner reserve contributions |
| **Reserve Adequacy Analysis** | Forecast future maintenance needs vs. available reserves |
| **Maintenance Planning** | Schedule and budget for planned maintenance and repairs |
| **DATEV Integration** | Export operating costs and reserves to accounting systems |

## Owner Reserve Management

### Reserve Contribution Calculation

**Methods:**
- Fixed amount per unit per month
- Percentage of operating costs
- Square meter-based calculation
- Usage-based (for commercial units)

**Reserve Types:**
- **Administrative Reserve (Verwaltungsrücklage)** — For minor repairs and cash flow smoothing
- **Maintenance Reserve (Instandhaltungsrücklage)** — For planned maintenance and major repairs
- **Capital Reserve (Kapitalrücklage)** — For major renovations and replacements

### Reserve Tracking

**Per Property:**
- Total reserves collected
- Reserves spent/allocated
- Available reserve balance
- Reserve adequacy ratio

**Per Owner:**
- Individual contribution history
- Share of reserves used
- Outstanding contributions
- Reserve statements

## Reserve Adequacy Analysis

### Maintenance Planning

**Long-term Planning (10-30 years):**
- Roof replacement
- Facade renovation
- Elevator modernization
- Heating system replacement
- Window replacement
- Electrical system updates

**Cost Estimation:**
- Current replacement values
- Inflation adjustments
- Cost escalation factors
- Regional price differences

### Adequacy Calculation

**Reserve Adequacy Ratio:**
```
Available Reserves / Estimated Future Maintenance Costs = Adequacy %
```

**Traffic Light System:**
- 🟢 Green: >80% — Reserves adequate
- 🟡 Yellow: 50-80% — Reserves need attention
- 🔴 Red: <50% — Critical reserve shortfall

### Scenario Planning
- Best case (low inflation, minimal repairs)
- Expected case (normal maintenance)
- Worst case (major failures, cost overruns)
