## §249 HGB Compliance

### Contract Valuation (Bilanzierung nach §249 HGB)

Construction contracts in Germany require special accounting treatment under §249 of the German Commercial Code:

**Percentage of Completion Method:**
- Revenue recognition based on project progress
- Cost-to-cost method for completion percentage
- Anticipated losses recognized immediately

**Key Calculations:**
- **Project Costs Incurred** / **Total Estimated Costs** = Completion %
- **Contract Price** × **Completion %** = Revenue Recognized
- **Work in Progress** tracking for balance sheet

### Balance Sheet Impact

| Account | Treatment |
|---------|-----------|
| **Work in Progress (Assets)** | Costs + recognized profit - invoiced amounts |
| **Advance Payments (Liabilities)** | Customer prepayments exceeding WIP |
| **Contract Receivables** | Invoiced amounts not yet paid |
