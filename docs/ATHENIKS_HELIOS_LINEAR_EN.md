## Atheniks Helios — Financial Intelligence for Renewable Energy Operators

### Short Description
Helios is the SaaS platform for plant-level P&L, EEG tariff management, project calculation, and automated lender reporting — specifically developed for solar, wind, and biogas operators in the DACH region (5–50 assets).

---

### Problem
Operators manage finances, EEG tariffs, and banking relationships with Excel. This costs:
- **2–3 days** for a lender report (quarterly)
- **Up to €15,000/year** from missed EEG deadlines (per 1 MWp)
- **€20,000+** in legal costs from covenant breaches

---

### Solution: 9 Integrated Modules

**PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT**
- Asset register with master data, MaStR ID, warranty tracking
- EEG tariff management for generations 2000–2024 with automated alerts (12/6/3 months before expiry)
- Fixed assets & depreciation: Linear/declining balance, maintenance history

**FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT**
- Plant-level P&L: Automated revenue calculation (kWh × tariff), cost allocation, monthly EBITDA
- Lender reporting: DSCR/LTV tracking, covenant dashboard (green/yellow/red), one-click PDF in banking standard
- Contracts & provisions: Central register, automated maturity management

**PLANNING & ANALYSIS**
- Project calculator: PVGIS-based yield forecasts, 20-year cash flow, break-even calculation

**DATA & DOCUMENTS**
- Integrations: Solar-Log API, SMA API, grid operator CSV, MaStR API, DATEV/SAP export
- AI-powered document storage with automatic categorization
